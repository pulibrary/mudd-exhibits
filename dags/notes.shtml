This exhibit was designed to be viewed at a screen resolution of 800x600 or larger.

Due to the dark coloring of these images and the variation in computer monitors, you may want to adjust the contrast and brightness of your monitor to obtain the best viewing experience. For fine-tuning your monitor, the web site "ZoneZero" has a helpful calibration page.

External links in this exhibit will open a new browser window.

Be sure print settings are set to "black text," if printing pages from this exhibit using Netscape. The option to print black text is found under the menu item File, Page Setup.
