Triple portrait of Alexander H. Phillips (Class of 1851), Hugh W. Henry (Class of 1851), and William Wallace Phillips, ca. 1850s. Half-plate daguerreotype. Photographer unknown. [After Treatment]

 

 

Once the daguerreotype plate was electro-cleaned, however, and the corrosion removed the three young men emerge with an amazing clarity. From their eyes and hair cowlicks, to their hands and clothing, the detail is incredible.
