H-bomb Issues
During the 1956 presidential campaign, Stevenson called for an end to aboveground nuclear weapons testing. His opponents dubbed this move as "politically opportunistic." However, Stevenson assembled a group of national experts who supported his views and bought thirty minutes of commercial television time to air their opinions. At the close of the show, Stevenson made this brief appearance to reinforce his viewpoint. Though unsuccessful in the election, his initiative would become reality in 1963 under the Kennedy administration.
