Madly for Adlai
This ditty by Stu Watson is but one of several records found in the Stevenson Papers. Others include: We Want Adlai: He Knows the Score (1952), The Man With the Hole in his Shoe (1956), Believe in Stevenson (1956), and You Gotta Gotta Win This Time (1956).
