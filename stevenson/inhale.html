"Don’t Inhale!"
Stevenson appeared on NBC’s Meet the Press, March 30, 1952. In this clip, he discusses his non-declaration of his presidential candidacy with a good bit of humor and humility. Early in 1952, while Stevenson was still governor of Illinois, President Harry S. Truman proposed that he seek the Democratic nomination for president. Stevenson at first hesitated, arguing that he was committed to running for a second gubernatorial term. Despite his protestations, the delegates drafted him, and he accepted the nomination at the Democratic National Convention in Chicago that summer. Here, Stevenson attempts to duck, rather than meet, the press.
