Paix et Liberté exhibit | Seeley G. Mudd Manuscript Library, Princeton University

 

 

The images made available here are for use in conjunction with the press release issued by Princeton University on the Paix et Liberté exhibit in July 2002. These images may be used only in publicizing this exhibit.

Beyond news stories on the exhibit, permission to publish materials from the collection must be requested from the University Archivist and Curator of Public Policy Papers. The Library has no information on the status of literary rights for the work of others found in these papers; researchers are responsible for determining any question of copyright.

The complete rights and permissions policy and fees can be found at http://www.princeton.edu/~rbsc/research/rights.html
